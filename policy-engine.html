About This Assessment

مسبار السياسات (Policy Probe) is a comprehensive assessment engine that evaluates policy documents across:

Extraction → Quality (10 domains) → Feasibility (6 axes) → Benchmarking → Gap Analysis → Verdict

The system analyzes compliance with Saudi Arabia's Vision 2030, regulatory alignment, implementation feasibility, and strategic impact to provide actionable insights.
